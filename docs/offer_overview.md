# ITM30 Sprint — Offer Overview

## What It Is

The **AI Integraterz Market Expansion 30-Day Sprint** is a done-with-you program that builds a new revenue channel for an existing business in 30 days.

## Who It's For

- Business owners with an existing audience or customer base
- Entrepreneurs with a cold list they haven't reactivated
- Founders with one core offer who want to expand
- Operators who are ready to build a new product or enter a new market

## What Gets Built

In 30 days, clients get:
1. A new offer, product, or tool defined and live
2. An Alpha Founding Circle with initial members enrolled
3. A Three-Engine GTM Machine running
4. An AI VA managing day-to-day ops
5. A Sprint Handoff Package for month 2 and beyond

## The Three Plays

**Play 01 — Database Reactivation:** For businesses with large cold lists.
**Play 02 — Market Capitalization:** For businesses with one main offer and untapped audience segments.
**Play 03 — Relaunch + Rebrand:** For businesses that need new positioning or a new channel.
