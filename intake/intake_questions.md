# ITM30 Sprint — Business Expansion Intake Questions

Work through these sections one at a time. The goal is to identify the highest-leverage play and build a custom 30-day plan around it. Summarize what you learn after each section before moving to the next.

---

## SECTION 1: The Business

1. What is your business — what do you sell, who do you sell it to, and at what price?
2. How long have you been running it? What stage are you at?
3. What's currently working? What's your primary revenue driver right now?
4. What does your audience look like — size, platform, how you acquired them?
5. What's your current monthly revenue range? (Ballpark is fine.)
6. What does "winning" look like for you in the next 90 days?

---

## SECTION 2: The Untapped Revenue

7. How big is your email list or CRM database? How engaged is it?
8. What percentage of leads convert to customers? What happens to the rest?
9. Have you done database reactivation campaigns before? What happened?
10. What adjacent products or services do your customers ask for that you don't offer?
11. What's the main reason people don't buy from you — price, timing, wrong fit, or something else?
12. Are there segments of your audience you've never specifically targeted with an offer?

---

## SECTION 3: The Competitive Landscape

13. Who are your main competitors? What are they doing that's working?
14. What do you do that they can't or don't? What's your actual differentiator?
15. Is your market getting crowded? Are you seeing increased competition?
16. Are there adjacent niches your audience overlaps with that you haven't entered?
17. What do your best customers say is the reason they chose you over everyone else?

---

## SECTION 4: The Play

*(After reviewing the above answers, identify which play applies. Ask these to confirm.)*

18. Have you thought about launching a secondary product, tool, or lower-priced offer to capture more of your audience? What's stopped you?
19. If you could launch one new thing in the next 30 days, what would it be?
20. Is there a free version, light version, or gateway product that could reactivate cold leads?
21. Have you considered expanding into a new niche, channel, or market with your current expertise?
22. Are there any software tools, apps, or platforms you wish existed for your customers?

---

## SECTION 5: The Audience + Outreach

23. Where does your audience hang out — LinkedIn, Instagram, email, communities, events?
24. What outreach channels have you tried — cold email, paid ads, LinkedIn, content?
25. What's worked best so far for generating leads or meetings?
26. Who are the top influencers, thought leaders, or voices your audience already trusts?
27. Do you have a warm list of people who fit the profile for the new product/offer?

---

## SECTION 6: GTM + Distribution

28. What cold outreach channel is strongest for your audience — email, LinkedIn, or both?
29. Do you have (or can you get) a prospect list for the new offer? What does it look like?
30. What messaging angle would stop your target audience and make them engage?
31. Are there any podcasts, communities, or events where your audience concentrates?
32. Do you have existing content, a newsletter, or social presence? What's been working?

---

## SECTION 7: The Expert Series + Authority

33. What's the authority topic your audience most wants to hear about right now?
34. Who would be ideal Expert Series guests for the new offer's positioning?
35. Do you have existing relationships with potential guests or collaborators?
36. What platform fits best for the interview content — audio, video, LinkedIn Live?

---

## SECTION 8: The AI VA

37. What tasks does the new product/offer require someone to run day-to-day?
38. What hours and timezone works for your VA?
39. How technical does your VA need to be?
40. Any specific tools they must know — your CRM, email platform, automations?

---

## SECTION 9: Logistics

41. What is your target sprint start date?
42. Preferred communication channel during the sprint?
43. Any hard deadlines, launches, or events we need to build around?
44. How do you want to allocate the $2,000 tools and ad budget?
45. Anything else we should know before we start?
