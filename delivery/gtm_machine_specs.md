# GTM Machine Specs — ITM30 Sprint

Live by Day 30. Zero manual work required after setup.

---

## System 1: Cold Email (16-Agent Stack)
**Output:** 25–50 qualified meetings/month
**Books directly to:** Founder's Calendly

### What Gets Built
- Target audience list (ICP-matched, verified emails)
- Multi-step sequence: hook email → value follow-up → final CTA
- 16-agent automation: list build → personalization → send → follow-up → booking
- Deliverability infrastructure: warmed domains, authenticated inboxes

### What We Need From the Client
- Ideal customer profile (from intake)
- Any existing prospect lists
- Calendly link
- Tone preferences and any topics to avoid

### Sequence Structure
- **Email 1 (Day 1):** Short hook — specific pain point, credibility, 1 soft CTA
- **Email 2 (Day 3):** Value add — share something useful, no pitch
- **Email 3 (Day 7):** Direct ask — book a call, clear offer
- **Email 4 (Day 14):** Final follow-up — breakup email, last chance

### Configuration Notes
- Send volume: start at 50/day, scale to 200–500/day by Week 4
- Sending windows: Tue–Thu, 8am–11am local time (highest open rates)
- Reply handling: AI Integraterz trains VA on how to respond

## System 2: LinkedIn Autopilot (37-Agent Stack)
**Output:** 10,000+ connections/month, zero manual engagement
**Voice:** Trained on founder's writing style

### What Gets Built
- Connection request sequences (target: ICP-matched profiles)
- Auto-comment system (engages with target influencers 2x daily)
- Auto-responder (scans inbound comments, detects leads, responds)
- Content scheduler (posts founder content on cadence)
- Campaign analyzer (weekly performance grading, adjusts strategy)

### What We Need From the Client
- 3–5 example posts or pieces of writing in the founder's voice
- List of 10 target influencers to engage with
- ICP filters for connection targeting (title, industry, company size, location)
- Content topics and any off-limits areas

### Voice Training
The LinkedIn AI is trained on a blend of proven copywriting styles, weighted to match the founder. Baseline blend (adjust per client):
- 45% Storytelling + earned authority (slow reveal, real experience)
- 20% Contrarian hooks + value stacking
- 15% Intent-based leadership (questions that reframe)
- 10% The "why" connector
- 10% Strategic positioning + lifetime value thinking

## System 3: Expert Series + Content Machine (18 Agents)
**Output:** 52+ pieces of content per month from 1 interview
**Distribution:** Every channel — LinkedIn, email, threads, video scripts

### What Gets Built
- Expert Series interview framework and guest brief
- Recording setup and post-production workflow
- 18-agent content machine: transcription → extraction → reformatting → distribution
- Content calendar for LinkedIn, newsletter, and short-form

### What 1 Interview Produces
- 5–7 LinkedIn posts (full long-form)
- 1 newsletter edition
- 3–5 short-form threads or carousels
- 1–2 video scripts (reels, shorts)
- 1 written case study or thought leadership piece

### What We Need From the Client
- First Expert Series guest confirmed (founding circle member preferred)
- Recording platform preference (Riverside, Zoom, StreamYard, etc.)
- Newsletter platform (ConvertKit, Beehiiv, Substack, etc.)
- Podcast platform if publishing audio (Spotify, Apple, etc.)

### Expert Series Guest Brief Template
For each guest, provide:
- Name, title, background
- 3 topics they have genuine authority on
- Any stories or experiences worth drawing out
- The #1 thing the audience should take away
