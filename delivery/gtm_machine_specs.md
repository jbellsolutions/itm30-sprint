# Three-Engine GTM Machine — Specs

## Overview

The GTM Machine is a three-engine system that runs in parallel from Day 15 onward. Each engine serves a different acquisition function.

---

## Engine 01 — Authority Machine (Primary)

**What it is:** A content and credibility system built around your Expert Series.

**How it works:**
1. Interview 1–2 experts per week (guests your audience already trusts)
2. Pull clips, quotes, and highlights from each interview
3. Distribute across LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube Shorts, and email
4. Each piece of content drives back to the new offer

**Why it's primary:** Builds trust at scale without ad spend. Content compounds over time. Works even when you're not actively selling.

**AI VA role:** Books guests, edits clips, schedules posts, repurposes content.

**KPIs:** Interviews per month, content pieces published, inbound leads from content.

---

## Engine 02 — Database Reactivation (Fastest Revenue)

**What it is:** An email or SMS sequence that wakes up cold leads with a new offer.

**How it works:**
1. Segment your list: cold (90+ days no engagement), warm (30–90 days), active
2. Build a reactivation sequence: 5–7 emails over 10–14 days
3. Lead with value (free tool, insight, or resource) before the ask
4. Offer founding member access or a limited-time deal
5. Remove non-responders to protect deliverability

**Why it's fastest:** You already own this list. No ad spend. Revenue in days, not weeks.

**AI VA role:** Manages sequence, handles replies, tags responders, books calls.

**KPIs:** Open rate, reply rate, booked calls, reactivation conversions.

---

## Engine 03 — Outbound Machine (Bonus)

**What it is:** A cold outreach system targeting new prospects who don't know you yet.

**How it works:**
1. Build a targeted list (LinkedIn Sales Nav, Apollo, or Apify scraping)
2. Write a 3-step cold email sequence (hook → value → CTA)
3. Personalize at scale using AI tools
4. Test LinkedIn outreach in parallel
5. Route all replies to AI VA for follow-up

**Why it's bonus:** Takes 2–3 weeks to see results. Use after Engines 01 and 02 are running.

**AI VA role:** Manages list building, sends sequences, handles first replies, books calls.

**KPIs:** Open rate, reply rate, meeting booked rate, cost per meeting.

---

## AI VA Configuration

All three engines are operated by a trained AI VA:
- Works from inside your business (your tools, your accounts)
- Trained on your offer, audience, and voice in Week 1
- Manages day-to-day execution by Week 2
- Reports weekly with metrics and recommendations

**VA profile:** Part-time to full-time, $8–$12/hr, trained on AI tools including GoHighLevel, Instantly, LinkedIn Sales Nav, and content scheduling platforms.
