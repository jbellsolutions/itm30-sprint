# Founding Circle Playbook — ITM30 Sprint

Based on the AI Integraterz GTM framework and the proven founding circle mechanics.

---

## What the Founding Circle Is

A hand-selected group of 10–20 ideal early users who test the offer, shape its development, and become the core referral and case study engine at public launch. Not a beta test. A founding tribe.

---

## The Ground Rules

- **Capacity:** 10–20 seats max. Hand-selected by the founder. No open applications.
- **The Offer:** Lifetime free access (or founding discount) + direct input on the product + white-glove onboarding + founding partner status
- **The Ask:** Actually use it. Give honest feedback. Participate in 2 interviews (Expert Series + testimonial).
- **The Payoff:** At launch, founding members become active referral engines — 20% recurring commission on any paid users they bring in.

---

## Step-by-Step Mechanics

### Step 1 — Build the List (Days 4–5)
- Founder identifies 20–30 potential founding circle members from their network
- Profile: ideal client avatar + people who will actually use it + people with reach
- AI Integraterz reviews the list and helps prioritize

### Step 2 — Send the Invite (Days 6–7)
- Founder sends friend-to-friend personal email to each person (see `/templates/founding_circle_invite.md`)
- NOT a mass email. Each one is personal and direct.
- LinkedIn DM or text works too — whatever is most natural for that relationship

### Step 3 — Private Application Page (Day 8–9)
- Simple `/alpha` page live — application form
- Form collects: name, background, why they want in, what they'll use it for
- Founder reviews applications personally

### Step 4 — White-Glove Onboarding (Days 10–11)
- Accepted members receive welcome email with unique referral link
- 1-on-1 onboarding call (15–20 min) with each member
- They're set up, active, and have their first task

### Step 5 — Expert Series Interview (Days 10–14)
- Founder records 20–30 min podcast/interview with each founding circle member
- Focus: their story, expertise, authority — NOT a product pitch
- Builds their profile + creates content for the brand's Expert Series

### Step 6 — Active Usage + Feedback (Days 14–21)
- Founding circle members actively using the offer
- Weekly check-in (async) — what's working, what isn't
- Feedback used to refine the offer before public launch

### Step 7 — Testimonial/Case Study (Days 21–28)
- Once a member gets a result, record a 15–20 min testimonial video
- Written case study produced from the video
- Used in public launch materials

### Step 8 — Public Launch Activation (Day 30+)
- Founding members' names on the public Founding Wall
- Referral links activated — 20% recurring commission
- They share launch announcement to their networks

## The Founding Circle Offer (Template)

Customize this for each client's specific offer:

**What they get:**
- [Lifetime free access / Founding price of $X / % discount forever]
- Direct input on what gets built
- First access to every new feature or phase
- Founding partner status at public launch
- Their Expert Series interview published and promoted

**What you ask:**
- Use the offer actively during the sprint
- Give honest feedback monthly (short form or quick call)
- Participate in Expert Series interview (20–30 min)
- Participate in testimonial interview once you have results (15–20 min)

---

## LinkedIn Announcement (Framework)

Post this after the first 5 founding circle members are confirmed. Customize tone and story to match the founder's voice.

**Structure:**
1. Open with a tension or story from the founder's past
2. Connect it to what they're building and why
3. Announce the founding circle — exclusive, limited seats
4. State clearly what members get and what's asked
5. Call to action: comment or DM, NOT a link (drives algorithm)
