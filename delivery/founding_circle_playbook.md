# Alpha Founding Circle — Playbook

## What It Is

The Alpha Founding Circle is a **founding member group** for the new offer. It runs in Phase 02 (Days 8–14) and serves three purposes:

1. **Validates the offer** with real paying customers (or engaged free members)
2. **Generates social proof and testimonials** for the GTM Machine
3. **Creates community** around the new product before it fully launches

---

## Who Gets Invited

The founding circle is recruited from:

- **Warm contacts** — existing customers, past leads, referral partners
- **Cold list** — people who opted in but never bought (reactivation play)
- **Social audience** — followers who've engaged with your content
- **Personal network** — people who know and trust you

Target: 10–50 founding members depending on product type.

---

## Founding Circle Offer

Founders get access at a **discounted or free rate** in exchange for:
- Feedback and active participation
- A testimonial or case study at the end
- Permission to feature their results in marketing

**Typical structure:**
- Free: for community-style or SaaS tools in beta
- 50–80% off: for coaching, courses, or services
- Pay-what-you-can: for info products testing price sensitivity

---

## Recruitment Sequence

**Day 8:** Send personal invite to warm list (email + DM)
**Day 9–10:** Follow up with non-openers
**Day 11:** Open to cold list / social audience with "founding member" framing
**Day 12–13:** Last call messaging (urgency + scarcity)
**Day 14:** Close founding circle, announce initial members

---

## How It Feeds the GTM Machine

- **Authority Machine:** Founding members become case studies and guests
- **Database Reactivation:** Cold list converts through founding circle offer
- **Outbound Machine:** Founding member testimonials become cold outreach social proof

---

## Success Metrics

- 10+ founding members enrolled by Day 14
- At least 3 members providing active feedback
- 1+ testimonial captured by Day 21
- Founding circle used as proof in GTM messaging by Day 22
