# ITM30 Sprint — Phase-by-Phase Delivery Plan

---

## PHASE 01 — Days 1–7: Offer + Niche Sprint

**Goal:** Offer locked. Niche confirmed. Prototype live. AI VA search underway.

### Day 1 — Genius Tap Call
- [ ] Welcome email sent
- [ ] Genius Tap Call completed (see `/templates/genius_tap_agenda.md`)
- [ ] Full intake captured
- [ ] Founder workspace set up (Notion / ClickUp)
- [ ] Offer + niche statement drafted on the call

### Days 2–3 — Offer Architecture
- [ ] Offer positioned: who it's for, what it does, why it's different
- [ ] Unique mechanism defined and named
- [ ] Pricing structure confirmed (founding circle offer vs. public offer)
- [ ] Expert Series framework mapped — topic, guest profile, format

### Days 4–5 — Prototype Build
- [ ] Prototype spec confirmed with founder
- [ ] Build begins — simplest working version of the offer
- [ ] Tools and platforms set up (whatever the offer runs on)
- [ ] AI VA search brief written and search begins

### Days 6–7 — Prototype Live
- [ ] Prototype functional and live
- [ ] Founder reviews and approves
- [ ] Founding circle outreach list drafted (10–20 target names)
- [ ] Friend-to-friend invite drafted (see `/templates/founding_circle_invite.md`)

**Phase 01 Deliverables:** Working prototype, Offer + niche statement, Expert Series framework, Founding circle list + invite draft

---

## PHASE 02 — Days 8–14: Prototype + Alpha Circle

**Goal:** AI VA hired and onboarded. Alpha Founding Circle built. Application + waitlist live. Business OS deployed.

### Days 8–9 — AI VA Onboarding
- [ ] AI VA candidates reviewed and selected
- [ ] Intro call: founder + VA + AI Integraterz
- [ ] VA briefed on offer, tools, daily responsibilities
- [ ] VA begins working — first tasks assigned

### Days 10–11 — Founding Circle Launch
- [ ] Friend-to-friend invite emails sent by founder (personal, 1-to-1)
- [ ] LinkedIn founding circle announcement post drafted and scheduled
- [ ] Private application page live (`/alpha` or equivalent)
- [ ] First founding circle members applying and onboarded

### Days 12–13 — Business OS + Automations
- [ ] Business OS deployed (Notion / ClickUp workspace fully built out)
- [ ] Core automations installed:
  - Lead capture → CRM tagging
  - Waitlist signup → welcome sequence
  - Calendly → confirmation + prep sequence
- [ ] Alpha offer packaged with pricing and terms

### Day 14 — Phase 02 Close
- [ ] Founding circle: minimum 5 members onboarded
- [ ] AI VA active and running daily tasks
- [ ] Application + waitlist system live
- [ ] Business OS operational

**Phase 02 Deliverables:** AI VA placed + onboarded, Founding circle (5+ members), Application/waitlist live, Business OS + automations

---

## PHASE 03 — Days 15–21: GTM Build

**Goal:** Cold email system built and tested. LinkedIn autopilot activated. Expert Series live. Content machine running. Landing page, deck, and one-pager complete.

### Days 15–16 — Cold Email System
- [ ] Target audience list built (see `/delivery/gtm_machine_specs.md`)
- [ ] Cold email copy written — hook, body, CTA
- [ ] 16-agent email system configured and tested
- [ ] Domain and inbox warmup confirmed (if new domains)
- [ ] First test sequence sent to small batch

### Days 17–18 — LinkedIn Autopilot
- [ ] 37-agent LinkedIn stack configured for founder's voice
- [ ] Connection request sequences loaded
- [ ] Auto-comment and engagement sequences activated
- [ ] Content calendar loaded for Weeks 3–4

### Days 19–20 — Expert Series + Content Machine
- [ ] Expert Series recording #1 completed with founding circle member
- [ ] 18-agent content machine processes interview:
  - LinkedIn posts (5–7 from one interview)
  - Email newsletter
  - Short-form threads
  - Video scripts (if applicable)
- [ ] Content distribution plan activated

### Day 21 — Phase 03 Close
- [ ] Landing page, sales deck one-pager live
- [ ] All GTM channels built and ready to launch
- [ ] VA handling content scheduling and distribution

**Phase 03 Deliverables:** Cold email system, LinkedIn autopilot, Expert Series ep. 1, Content machine live, Landing page + one-pager

---

## PHASE 04 — Days 22–30: Go Live

**Goal:** Full GTM machine turned on. Cold email sending. LinkedIn running. Alpha circle onboarded with first revenue. VA certified. Sprint closed.

### Days 22–24 — GTM Machine On
- [ ] Cold email at full send volume (target: 200–500/day depending on list)
- [ ] LinkedIn autopilot running at full capacity
- [ ] Content publishing daily across all channels
- [ ] Founder tracking meetings booked + pipeline building

### Days 25–27 — Alpha Circle Close + First Revenue
- [ ] All founding circle members onboarded and active
- [ ] Alpha offer presented to founding circle — first paid conversions
- [ ] Testimonial/case study conversations started with active members
- [ ] Referral links activated for founding circle (20% recurring commission)

### Days 28–29 — VA Certification + Handoff Prep
- [ ] AI VA certified on all systems — runs the full GTM machine independently
- [ ] Handoff document drafted (see `/templates/handoff_doc.md`)
- [ ] All sprint deliverables finalized and organized in client workspace

### Day 30 — Sprint Close
- [ ] Final sprint review call (45 min)
- [ ] GTM machine performance review: meetings booked, pipeline, connections
- [ ] VA certified and handed off to founder
- [ ] After-sprint offer presented ($500/mo · $1,000/mo · $1,000+20%)
- [ ] Sprint closed

**Phase 04 Deliverables:** Full GTM machine running, Alpha circle revenue, VA certification, Handoff doc, Sprint closed
