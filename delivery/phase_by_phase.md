# ITM30 Sprint — Phase-by-Phase Delivery

## Overview

The sprint runs in 4 phases across 30 days. Each phase has a clear goal, set of deliverables, and checkpoint.

---

## Phase 01 — Product Sprint (Days 1–7)

**Goal:** Define and build the new product, offer, or tool.

**Deliverables:**
- Business audit completed (Day 1 discovery call)
- Play selected and confirmed (DB Reactivation / Market Cap / Rebrand)
- New offer defined: name, price, promise, audience
- Landing page or waitlist page live
- Day 7 delivery asset complete (tool, PDF, mini-course, or offer page)

**Checkpoint:** Can we describe the offer in one sentence and show a live URL?

---

## Phase 02 — Audience Activation (Days 8–14)

**Goal:** Activate existing audience and warm network.

**Deliverables:**
- Alpha Founding Circle recruitment launched
- Founding Circle invite sequence sent (warm list first)
- Initial 10–25 founding members enrolled or on waitlist
- Expert Series guest #1 booked or in outreach
- Database reactivation email sequence live (if Play 01)

**Checkpoint:** Have we gotten real people to raise their hand?

---

## Phase 03 — GTM Machine (Days 15–21)

**Goal:** Launch the three-engine go-to-market system.

**Deliverables:**
- Engine 01: Authority Machine — first Expert Series interview recorded or scheduled
- Engine 01: Content clips scheduled across LinkedIn/Instagram/YouTube
- Engine 02: Database Reactivation — full sequence live, responses being handled
- Engine 03: Outbound Machine — cold email or LinkedIn outreach live (if applicable)
- AI VA onboarded and running day-to-day ops

**Checkpoint:** Is the machine running without the owner?

---

## Phase 04 — Scale + Handoff (Days 22–30)

**Goal:** Optimize what's working, hand off to VA, and set up for month 2.

**Deliverables:**
- First revenue from new offer confirmed (or pipeline built)
- GTM system optimized based on Week 3 data
- AI VA running independently
- Month 2 plan drafted
- Sprint handoff document delivered

**Checkpoint:** Is this business generating new revenue from a new channel?

---

## Day 7 Guarantee

By Day 7, every client has:
1. A new product, offer, or tool defined and live
2. A clear play identified and confirmed
3. Audience activation begun

If we haven't delivered these three things by Day 7, we'll refund 100% — no questions asked.
